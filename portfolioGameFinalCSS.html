The Deck

In this game, there six suites. They include Hearts, Diamonds, Spades, Clubs and two new suites of Skulls and Swords.Each suite has the normal card ranks (2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, Jack, Queen, King, Ace) with Ace being a high card following King to make up a deck of 84 cards.

The Hands

You will go through 14 hands by clicking the "show next hand" button. When you see the five cards of your hand, click on two. Those cards will be sent to your collection which can be seen below your hand.

Changing Collection Order

At any time, you can go down to your collection and click on two cards. Two additional buttons will show up next to the "Go To Confirmation Stage" button, the "Swap" and "Reset" buttons. If you click the "Reset" button, the buttons will disappear and nothing will happen. If you click the "Swap " button, the two cards will switch positions.

Scoring and Confirmation

The goal of the game is to score the most points. Click the see point values to see how much points each card combination is worth. At the end of the game, when your collection is in the order you wish click the "Go To Confirmation Stage" button. At this point you will be unable to change the position of the cards in your collection. Two more buttons will appear, the "Evaluate Sets" and "Get Final Score" buttons. Clicking on the "Evaluate Sets" button will calculate your final score based on the first 25 cards of your collection. The first set of 5 will evaluate to add a point value to your total score. The second, third, fourth, and fifth sets will do the same. Then click on the "Get Final Score" button to see your total final score.

You will get awarded a certain amount of points for each hand depending on the highest card combination attained. These are listed below.

  1. Royal Flush (five cards same suit, values of 10, Jack, Queen, King, and Ace in that sequence): 80 points
  2. Straight Flush (five cards same suit, five cards with sequential rank): 60 points
  3. Four of a Kind (four cards with same rank): 50 points
  4. Full House (three cards with same rank and two other cards with same rank): 40 points
  5. Flush (five cards with same suit): 35 points
  6. Straight (five cards with sequential rank): 30 points
  7. Three of a Kind (three cards with same rank): 25 points
  8. Two Pair (two cards with same rank and another two cards with same rank): 15 points
  9. One Pair (two cards with same rank): 10 points
